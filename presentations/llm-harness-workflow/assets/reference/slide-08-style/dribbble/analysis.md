## Useful Directions

1. Workflow canvas references are stronger than generic presentation templates for Slide 8.
2. The best fit is a node-editor language: compact nodes, faint rails, subtle grouping areas, and controlled density.
3. Process infographic references are useful for `계획 / 설계 / 구현 / 검증`, but their bright colors should be reduced to avoid breaking the deck tone.

## Application To Slide 8

- Keep the `Question / LLM / Answer` structure.
- Make question and answer placeholders feel like connected nodes, not speech bubbles.
- On grouping, show Q/A pairs as compact workflow nodes inside four quiet columns.
- Add thin connector rails and softer cards rather than more decorative bubbles.
